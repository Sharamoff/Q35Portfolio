PROJECT OVERVIEW

Based on technical drawings and detailed engineering documentation, the objective was to develop an accurate 3D model of the corner shower and present it within a realistic interior environment for marketing and promotional use.

THE CHALLENGE

Translate precise engineering drawings into a production-accurate 3D model.
Develop multiple material and hardware variations.
Place the product within a fully designed bathroom environment.
Produce high-quality visual assets and an animation suitable for advertising.

SOLUTION & EXECUTION

Built a detailed 3D model of the Q35 shower enclosure.
Developed 6 glass material variations and 6 hardware finish options.
Designed a complete bathroom interior to showcase the product in context.
Produced multiple still renderings from different angles.
Set up realistic lighting to enhance material quality and spatial depth.
Created a promotional animation for marketing presentation.
